The Story

Monkey Maghees is Poughkeepsie. For over 16 years, this legendary bar has been the heartbeat of the Hudson Valley. While countless neighborhood bars have shuttered across the Northeast—victims of changing demographics and competition—Monkey Maghees has not just survived; it has thrived.

It's a "last of its kind" venue in an era when authentic dive bars and neighborhood pubs are becoming extinct. That rarity, combined with consistent profitability and a fiercely loyal customer base, makes this an exceptional opportunity for the right buyer.

"This is the real deal. A genuine neighborhood bar with the revenue to prove it. No hype, no shortcuts—just a solid, profitable business with deep community roots."

Monkey Maghees operates a proven, sustainable model: happy hour Mon-Fri (5-7pm), pool tournaments, darts, live music on Fridays, and a loyal customer base that spans decades. Whether it's a Friday night packed with regulars or a casual Tuesday afternoon, the bar maintains consistent foot traffic and revenue.

The location is unbeatable. 40 Vassar Rd in a premium commercial complex with abundant parking and visibility. Proximity to Dunkin' and Rite Aid means constant foot traffic from neighboring businesses. The space itself—with its bar, pool area, kitchen, and event space—is configured for maximum operational efficiency and revenue generation.

This is a rare find: a profitable bar with real numbers to back it up, licenses already in place, a turnkey operation ready for immediate cash flow, and an owner willing to stick around to help with the transition.
